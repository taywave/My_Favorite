Fanpage : NEEDTOBREATHE

Statistics

  • NEEDTOBREATHE is an American Christian rock band from Seneca, South Carolina.
  • The band formed in 1998.
  • Their most popular song is Brother (feat. Gavin DeGraw) which has over 123 million streams on Spotify.

They’ve placed five Number One albums all across the Billboard chart spectrum, from Rock and Alternative. They’ve racked up two billion career streams and scored multi-platinum chart topping hits. And they’ve done it all while filling venues across the globe, either as headliners or with a diverse array of superstars like Taylor Swift, OneRepublic and Tim McGraw.
